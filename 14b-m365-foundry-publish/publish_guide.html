Publish Agent to Microsoft Teams

Complete the Teams integration for your Foundry agent

1 Publish in the Foundry Portal

Open your agent in the portal, then:

  • Click "Publish" button (top right)
  • Select "Publish to Teams"
  • Fill in metadata (description, developer, URLs)
  • Choose scope: Shared or Private
  • Click Publish
Open Foundry Portal Docs
✓ Step completed - now publish in the portal
The Publish button in the Microsoft AI Foundry portal

2 Find Your Agent in Teams

After publishing completes:

  • Open Microsoft Teams
  • Click Copilot in the sidebar
  • Click @ or type @
  • Search for "{{AGENT_NAME}}"
  • Select and start chatting
Find your agent via @ in Teams Copilot
It may take a few minutes for your agent to appear. Shared scope may require admin approval.
